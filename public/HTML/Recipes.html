My Chili Recipe

  • Home
  • Memes
  • Chili:
    • 1 Red Onion
    • 1 Red Bell Pepper
    • 2-3 Jalepenos
    • 3-5 Garlic Cloves
    • Bacon (optional)
    • Garlic, Onion, and Chili Powder
    • Cumin
    • Cayenne
    • Smoked Paprika
    • Salt + Pepper
    • Ground beef
    • Tomato Paste
    • Fire Roasted Tomatoes
    • 1 Can of Beer
    • 1-2 Can of Pinto Beans
    • 1 cup Beef Stock
    • 1 teaspoon of Cocao Powder
  • Toppings:
    • Green Onions
    • Sour Cream
    • Cheese

    • Steps:

    1. Dice Vegtables + Bacon
    2. Mix Half of seasoning mix with Ground Beef
    3. In the Pot, add bacon & render fat. Set coooked bacon aside, leave bacon grease in the pot
    4. Add Ground Beef to pot and cook until browned. Set Aside
    5. Add the rest of the seasonings in pot mix until fragrant
    6. add vegtables, fire roasted tomatoes, and tomato paste. mix well
    7. Add Beer to deglaze bottom of the pan
    8. Add drained pinto beans, bacon, and ground beef. Mix well
    9. Add beef stocj and cocao powder
    10. Simmer for about 20-20 minutes
    11. Top with cheddar cheese, sour cream, and green onions.
